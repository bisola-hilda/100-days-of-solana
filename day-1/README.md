# Day 1 – Generate Your First Solana Wallet

## Challenge

Generate your first Solana wallet using JavaScript and the Solana Kit SDK.

---

## What I Learned

- How public and private keypairs are generated.
- Every wallet has a unique public address.
- The private key proves ownership and must be kept secure.
- Solana wallets use Ed25519 cryptography.

## Lesson of the Day

A Solana wallet is simply a cryptographic keypair. The public key is your identity on the blockchain, while the private key allows you to sign transactions and prove ownership.
