# Day 7 – Sharing My Solana Journey

## Challenge

Share my wallet experiments publicly and engage with other participants in the #100DaysOfSolana community.

---

## What I Shared

This week I shared my journey of learning Solana wallets, generating my first keypair, connecting Phantom Wallet, and understanding how blockchain identity works.

## What I Learned

Learning in public is about more than documenting progress—it creates opportunities to receive feedback, connect with other developers, and reinforce my understanding by explaining concepts in my own words.

I also realized that the Solana community is very supportive, and engaging with others helps me discover different perspectives and solutions.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Learning publicly accelerates growth.
- Sharing progress builds confidence.
- Community feedback helps improve understanding.
- Explaining concepts reinforces learning.
- Consistency is more important than perfection.
