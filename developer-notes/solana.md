# Solana Notes

## What is Solana?

Solana is a high-performance Layer 1 blockchain designed for fast, secure, and low-cost transactions.

---

## Native Token

SOL

---

## Smallest Unit

1 SOL = 1,000,000,000 Lamports

---

## Wallet

A wallet manages cryptographic keys and allows users to sign transactions.

Examples:

- Phantom
- Solflare
- Backpack

---

## Keypair

A Solana identity consists of:

- Public Key (Wallet Address)
- Private Key (Proof of Ownership)

Never share your private key.

---

## RPC

RPC (Remote Procedure Call) allows applications to communicate with Solana nodes.

Examples:

- getBalance()
- getSignaturesForAddress()

---

## Programs

Programs are Solana's smart contracts that execute logic on-chain.
